His Inventions

1. Alternating Current (AC) System: Tesla's development of the AC system revolutionized electrical power generation, transmission, and distribution. His AC induction motor and transformer made it possible to transmit electricity over long distances efficiently.

2. Tesla Coil: This high-voltage, resonant transformer is used in many applications such as radio transmission, wireless power transfer, and medical devices.

3. Radio: While Guglielmo Marconi is often credited with the invention of radio, Tesla made crucial contributions to its development, including the invention of the Tesla coil, which enabled the transmission and reception of radio waves.

4. Remote Control: Tesla patented the first remote-controlled boat in 1898, demonstrating the principles of wireless communication and remote control.

5. Tesla Turbine: An unconventional turbine design utilizing smooth, closely spaced disks instead of blades. It has applications in power generation, propulsion systems, and pumps.Tesla Valve: A one-way flow valve with no moving parts, which he patented in 1920. It's used in various industries for fluid control.

6. Tesla Oscillator: A mechanical oscillator with applications in generating mechanical vibrations and studying resonance phenomena.
